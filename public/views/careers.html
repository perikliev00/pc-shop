Senior PC Technician

Location: Remote / Office

Type: Full-time

Experience: 3+ years

We're looking for an experienced PC technician to join our custom build team. You'll be responsible for assembling high-end gaming systems, troubleshooting hardware issues, and ensuring quality control.

Requirements:

  • Certification in PC hardware (A+, CompTIA, or equivalent)
  • Experience with custom PC building and overclocking
  • Knowledge of latest gaming hardware and compatibility
  • Strong problem-solving and communication skills

Customer Support Specialist

Location: Remote

Type: Full-time

Experience: 1+ years

Join our customer support team to help gamers build their dream PCs. You'll provide technical guidance, troubleshoot issues, and ensure customer satisfaction.

  • Basic knowledge of PC components and compatibility
